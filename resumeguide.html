Resume Writing Guide

A straightforward guide to help you build a high-impact resume that stands out in 2026.

01. Prioritize Readability

In modern recruitment, less is more. Recruiters scan quickly, so use clean fonts, generous margins, and a balanced layout that makes your strongest information easy to catch.

02. Use Reverse Chronological Order

Always list your most recent work experience or degree first. This helps recruiters understand your current level and most relevant strengths immediately.

Keep dates clearly formatted near each role or qualification.

03. Quantify Your Achievements

Do not only list responsibilities. Show measurable results where possible. For example, "Increased quarterly sales by 20%" is stronger than saying "Managed sales team."
