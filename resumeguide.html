Resume Writing Guide

A straightforward guide to help you build a high-impact resume that stands out in 2026.

01. Prioritize Readability

In modern recruitment, "Less is More" is the gold standard. Recruiters spend an average of only 6-7 seconds screening a resume. Always use clean fonts, generous margins, and a balanced layout to ensure your key information is caught instantly.

02. Master the Reverse Chronological Order

Always list your most recent work experience or degree at the top. This allows recruiters to immediately see your current level of seniority and most relevant skills.

Note: Ensure dates are clearly formatted on the right side or directly under your job title for quick scanning.

03. Quantify Your Achievements

Don't just list your "Responsibilities"—showcase your "Achievements." Use hard numbers to provide context. For example: "Increased quarterly sales by 20%" is far more powerful than simply saying "Managed sales team."
